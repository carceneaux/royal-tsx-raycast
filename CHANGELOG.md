# Royal TSX Changelog

## [Initial Release] - {PR_MERGE_DATE}

- 1.0.0
- 🎉 Initial release of the Royal TSX Raycast extension.
- 🔍 **Connection List** command: search and connect to connections from your open Royal TSX document.
- 🕐 Recent connections section tracks and surfaces the last 10 used connections.
- 🌐 Ad hoc connection support: connect to any hostname directly when no saved connection matches the search.
- ⚠️ Graceful error state displayed when Royal TSX is not running.
